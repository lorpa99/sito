Program Overview

The 37th Himalaya-Karakoram-Tibet Workshop will feature a variety of activities, including a short course, main workshop, social events, and a field trip. Below are the details of these events:

Short Course

“Microtectonics of Himalayan rocks: a tale of beauty” - September 1st, 2025 (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM). This event is reserved for students, PhD researchers, and early-career scientists (Max. 25 participants; first come-first served). Location: AULA POGNANTE, Earth Science Department, University of Torino.

More details available at University of Torino

Icebreaker Party

September 1st, 2025 at 18:00 - @ Museo Regionale di Scienze Naturali.

More details at Museo Regionale

Main Workshop

September 2nd-4th, 2025 - @ Aula Magna of Department of Life Sciences and Systems Biology. Thematic sessions will be based on the received abstracts, covering all topics related to the Himalaya, Karakoram, and Tibet.

More details available at Department of Life Sciences

Social Dinner

September 3rd, 2025 - @ Restaurant “Al Monte dei Cappuccini”.

More details at Restaurant Details

Field Trip

September 5th-6th, 2025 - Post-conference field trip along the W Alps.
