Abstracts are invited for both oral and poster presentations. Attendees may request, but are not guaranteed, an oral presentation. Both oral and poster presentations must be in English.

Abstract submission

The deadline for abstract submission is 30 April 2025 h. 19.00 CET

Abstracts should be sent via email to geologia.himalayana@unito.it following the attached template – Abstract TEMPLATE. Please, be aware no figures or tables are allowed in the Abstract – see Author Guide.

The abstract book will be published in the “Abstract book series" edited by the Geological Society of Italy.

A maximum of 2 contributions (1 for oral contribution - 1 for poster) are admitted for every workshop’s participant.

Guidelines for oral and poster presentations

Oral presentations should be arranged for 12+3 min (talk + Q&A).

Presentations should be prepared using commonly adopted formats (ppt, pptx, pdf) with slide aspect ratio of 16/9.

The presentation file should be uploaded on the PC in the conference room, at least 1 hour before the starting of the session.

Posters should be prepared in A0 portrait (841x1190 mm).
